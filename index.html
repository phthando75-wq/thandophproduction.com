About

DJy Thando PH is an Amapiano producer and DJ from Elsies River, Cape Town. Known for his signature blend of deep, soulful rhythms and energetic beats, he's been steadily carving out his space in the South African music scene.

  • Label: Dlomo Entertainment
  • Style: Soulful Amapiano with jazz and deep house textures
  • Base: Elsies River, Cape Town

"I make music that feels like home — soulful, rhythmic, and real. Amapiano is our language, and I'm just adding my dialect to it."
